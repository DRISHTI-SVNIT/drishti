ROVER

ROVER is a space exploration vehicle designed to witness the future of space exploration. These are used to traverse unexpected and unfamiliar terrains along with analyzing the possibility of life existence on different planets. Mars Society is a worldwide volunteer-driven space-advocacy non-profit organization dedicated for promoting the human exploration and settlement of the planet Mars. IRC (Indian Rover Challenge) is the only robotics and space exploration based competition in Asia organized by Mars Society to ignite and encourage the spirit of innovation amongst young minds.

Team DRISHTI has been working on the Rover since last three years and participating in IRC since last two years. We secured 4th rank in IRC 2018, 8th rank in IRC 2019 and 9th rank in IRC 2020.

ROBOCON

Robocon ( Asia Pacific robotic contest) is organised by the Asia Pacific broadcasting union (ABU). A collection of over 20 countries of Asia pacific region participate in the contest. Each year the competition has a different problem statement on the basis of hosting country's culture or global problems. The competition is held in 2 stages - the national stage which decides the team/college that would be representing the country in international competition (final stage) held in last week of August every year.

Team Drishti representing NIT, Surat has been participating in this competition since 2006 and has been setting a higher benchmark for itself, each year in the competition. In Robocon 2018 team Drishti ranked 12th in national stage among 108 teams nationwide.

AERO

Human pictured about the flying of birds and they started thinking about the thought to fly like them. It was 1903 when wright brothers did their first Flight Test and then aerodynamics became the hot chattered topic. From a car on the road to electricity through windmill everything involves the principles of aerodynamics.

Thus, to stimulate the development in the field of Aerodynamics, here in Drishti we study the aerodynamics and make it applicable in various aspects. We work on development of Different RC planes depending on their applications. We basically design, analyse and manufacture the RC planes. Since the developed technology of the quadcopter is expensive, we are developing our own Flight controller for the Quadcopter which can be further developed for autonomous RC Plane. We have also participated in Boeing Aeromodelling Competition. Considering that Aerodynamics is not only limited to Sky, we also work to develop our own Solid Propellent Rockets.

CHEMICAL

Chemical technology helps us study, develop, research and deploy all the statistical, theoretical and experimental facts behind the functioning and processes involved in the system. It ranges from the tasks as big as designing a nuclear reactor to as mini as measuring its surrounding temperature. Everything from nuclear fusion to modifying day to day materials comes under its study.

Team Drishti is also working on many aspects of Chemical Technology for the betterment of society. As the rate of energy consumption is continuously being increased in the recent years, scientists and engineers have to concentrate on developing new energy technologies and thermal management systems. Taking it as a challenge, Team Drishti has worked on Simulation of Heat Pipe for better Heat Transfer and Waste Heat Management in various industrial processes. Along with this, Team Drishti has also participated in the Chemical Engineering Techfest of IIT Bombay namely Azeotropy.
