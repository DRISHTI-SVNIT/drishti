TECHNOVATION

We present TECHNOVATION where we bring 4 exciting competitions from different domains each consisting of a problem statement which you will have to solve within the given time. Also, you will be assigned mentors who will guide you throughout the competition. There are no branch restrictions to participate in competitions. NOTE: Anchorage and Chemfluence are individual events. Solatronics and Antigravity are Team events.Team (3-5 members) can have members from different branch. While registering for team only ONE member need to fill the form on behalf of the entire team. Don’t worry if you cannot find a team, contact us if you want to participate we will make the team for you. Fill the Google form again for each competition if participating in more than one competition.
