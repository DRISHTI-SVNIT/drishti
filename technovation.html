TECHNOVATION

We present TECHNOVATION where we bring 4 exciting competitions from different domains, each consisting of a problem statement which you will have to solve within the given time. Also, you will be assigned mentors who will guide you throughout the competition. There are no branch restrictions to participate in competitions.
NOTE: Individual participation is required in Anchorage and Chemfluence. Solatronics and Antigravity are Team events. Team (3-5 members) can have members from different branches. While registering for the team, only ONE member needs to fill the form on behalf of the entire team. Don’t worry if you cannot find a team and want to participate; contact us, we will make the team for you. Fill the Google form again for each competition if participating in more than one competition.
