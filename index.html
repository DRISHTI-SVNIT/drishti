Welcome To Drishti



A place where "WE" comes before "ME". Drishti is a technical club of our institute where we strive to work with excellence and motivation. Drishti has been the driving force in igniting the young minds of SVNIT in taking up various technical projects. It boasts of providing a platform to the students where they can collaborate, carry out technical projects and can see their most creative and innovative ideas, and thus their dreams materialize.
